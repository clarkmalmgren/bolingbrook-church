Tithing is the beginning of giving

The Bible talks of tithing as the beginning point for giving. The word tithe literally means a tenth, that is 10% of our income. When we give the first 10% of our income to the church, we put God first in our lives and we help spread the story of His love. In fact, tithing is more than just giving something to God, it is giving back to God what He already gave to us. Of course, there is no way we can ever repay God for the ultimate gift of His Son on the cross, but in tithing we begin to express our gratitude, trust, and worship to Him who has given us all.

Moreover, our tithe helps support the global mission of the Seventh Day Adventist church and its Pastors.
